Syrnyky

Description

Syrnyky are Ukrainian cheesecakes, cooked as fried pancakes. This is a light, protein-rich dessert made from cottage cheese (tvorog), eggs, flour/semolina, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • cottage cheese - 400-500 g
  • 1 egg
  • sugar - 3 tbsp.
  • vanilla sugar - 10 g
  • flour - 100 g
  • a pinch of salt
  • sunflower oil for frying

Steps

  1. Thoroughly mix cottage cheese, egg, sugar, vanilla sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add flour to the bowl and mix thoroughly into homogeneous dough like mass is formed.
  3. Sprinkle some flour on a plate or board.
  4. Grease hands with a bit of sunflower oil and form cheesecakes as small 1/3 hand size cakes, 1.5 - 2 cm height. Roll cheesecakes in the flour from each side.
  5. Fry syrnyky on a frying pan with sunflower oil from each side, medium heat, until golden-brown color. Approx. 2 min.
  6. Place the finished syrnyky on a paper towel to absorb excess fat.
  7. Transfer syrnyky on plates and serve warm.
  8. Can be served with toppings such as sour cream or jam.
