Semolina Porridge

Description

Semolina porridge is a creamy, mild, and fast-cooking hot breakfast cereal made by simmering semolina flour (coarsely ground durum wheat) in milk or water.

Ingredients

  • Milk - 600 ml
  • Semolina flour - 3 tbsp.
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp.
  • Butter - 30 g

Steps

  1. Heat the milk in a saucepan, until it's hot but not boil, reduce heat to low.
  2. Mix semolina flour with sugar.
  3. Gently pour the mix into milk, stirring constantly.
  4. Continue stirring for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat, (optionally) add the butter and let it stay for 3-5 minutes.
  6. The dish is ready.
  7. You can play around with density by adding less or more semolina flour.
